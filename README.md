# shipshop
A smart grocery application that shows you where can cut spending and save some cash. 

## Motivation
The devleopment of Shipshop is based off my own struggles to see my grocery bill rise and rise even when I try to cut spending. 

- Why should you beholden to the most convienent grocery store because you use it the most often? 
- Wouldn't you like to see how much you spend on meat or snacks in a month?
- What if you could compare the egg prices in your area and decide it's not worth the effort to save $1. That choice should be in your hands.

## Quick Start

1. Go to [shipshop.kenzojohnson.dev](https://shipshop.kenzojohnson.dev) and create an account
2. Save money!

## Usage
A few features that might appeal to your sensibilities
- See your spending across time (that's not one grocery store dependent)
- Track individual purchases of snacks
- Visualize that data
- Compare pricing of individual items and conceptual items across services (Kroger, HEB, Publix, etc.)
